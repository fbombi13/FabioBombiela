Presentation on the application of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in software testing, covering AI-powered test generation, intelligent test optimization, and anomaly detection in test results.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to AI/ML in testing
- AI-powered test case generation
- Intelligent test prioritization
- Anomaly detection in test execution
- Computer vision for UI testing
- Future trends and opportunities

## Key Takeaways

- AI augments human testing expertise
- ML models can optimize test suites by 30-40%
- Importance of quality training data
- Human-in-the-loop approach for best results

## Audience

Testing professionals, automation engineers, and developers interested in AI applications in QA.
