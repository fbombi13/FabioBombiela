Mentoring program to help QA professionals prepare for ISTQB Foundation Level certification.

## Program Structure

### Study Sessions
- Weekly study group meetings
- Coverage of all ISTQB syllabus topics
- Practice questions and mock exams
- Discussion of real-world applications

### Topics Covered
- Fundamentals of testing
- Testing throughout the software lifecycle
- Static testing
- Test design techniques
- Test management
- Tool support for testing

## Mentoring Approach

- Personalized study plans
- One-on-one mentoring sessions
- Group discussions and knowledge sharing
- Practice exam reviews
- Tips and strategies for exam success

## Results

- Improved testing practices in projects
- Enhanced team capabilities

## Resources Provided

- Study guides and summaries
- Practice question banks
- Mock exams
- Reference materials
- Exam tips and strategies
